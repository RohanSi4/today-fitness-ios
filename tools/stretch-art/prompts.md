# Stretch illustration prompts

One tuned prompt per stretch, all sharing a single house style so the set
looks cohesive. Paste each into your image generator, then save the result as
`out/stretch-<id>.png` and run `python3 generate.py --install-local out`.

**Shared style** (already baked into every prompt below):
> Minimal flat vector illustration of a single athletic figure, *<pose>*.
> Solid deep-green figure (#29704F) on a fully transparent background, thick
> rounded strokes, clean simple fitness-diagram style, centered, shown from the
> angle that reads clearest, no text, no background elements, square composition.

## Dynamic warm-up

- **stretch-butt-kickers** — jogging forward while flicking one heel up toward the glute
- **stretch-frankensteins** — walking and kicking one straight leg up high in front to meet the opposite hand
- **stretch-scoop-toe-touches** — bending forward at the hips, both arms scooping down past the toes
- **stretch-open-close-gate** — standing on one leg, the other knee lifted and rotating outward to open the hip
- **stretch-carioca** — moving sideways with one foot crossing over in front of the other, hips rotated
- **stretch-walking-lunge-twist** — in a deep forward lunge with the torso rotated over the front knee
- **stretch-lateral-leg-swings** — one hand on a vertical pole, swinging one leg out to the side across the body
- **stretch-front-back-leg-swings** — one hand on a vertical pole, swinging one leg straight forward and back

## Static cool-down

- **stretch-wall-calf** — both hands pressed on a wall, one leg stepped back straight with the heel down, stretching the calf
- **stretch-crossed-toe-touch** — standing with ankles crossed, folding forward to reach the toes
- **stretch-wide-toe-touch** — standing in a wide stance, bending to reach toward one foot
- **stretch-crossed-side-bend** — legs crossed at the ankles, one arm reaching up and over to the side
- **stretch-standing-quad** — standing on one leg, holding the opposite foot behind the glute
- **stretch-seated-hamstring** — seated on the floor, one leg extended, reaching toward the foot
- **stretch-butterfly** — seated on the floor, soles of the feet together, knees dropped out to the sides
- **stretch-seated-twist** — seated on the floor, one leg crossed over the other, torso twisted
- **stretch-pigeon** — in pigeon pose, one shin folded forward on the floor, torso lowered over it
- **stretch-downward-calf** — in a downward-dog pike position, one heel pressing toward the floor to stretch the calf
